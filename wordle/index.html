How to Play

  1. Choose a word of five letters.
  2. Click "Guess" to see if the word is correct.
  3. You have six chances to guess the word.
  4. The letters in the correct position will be highlighted in green, while correct letters in the wrong position will be highlighted in yellow.
  5. Use the feedback to guess the word.
  6. Have fun!
